Next game night:

Fall 2017

This season, we'll be alternating between board games and a narrative campaign authored by your hosts (since we like acronyms and alliteration, let's go with... Storytelling & Saloons). Each episode of the campaign will use a different simple mechanic to help tell a standalone story within the season-long arc, making it easy for players to join for one, some, or all episodes.

Schedule

Tuesdays at 7:30pm

  • @ Soft Spot Bar
    (Bedford & N10th)
    • Sept 19 - ST&S Episode 1
      Title: Resting Beach Face / Core Mechanic: D6
    • Sept 26 - Rewordable
      Dust off your dictionary! Rewordable challenges players to mix together words with 1-3 letter chunks from your hand, a common pool, and even your opponents’ lexicon. It’s pretty much a chunky Bananagrams smoothie! (Learn more)
    • Oct 10 - ST&S Episode 2
      Title: Caged by the Bell / Core Mechanic: Battle via Funemployment cards
  • @ Reclamation Bar
    (Metropolitan & Bushwick)
    • Oct 17 - Killer Queen
      One game, Two teams, Three ways to win! Up to 10 players face off against each other in this unique action strategy arcade game. Whether you’re the queen or a drone, teamwork is the only path to victory. (Learn more)
  • @ Soft Spot Bar:
    • Oct 31 - ST&S Episode 3
      Title:The Newly Dead Game / Core Mechanic: Gameshow
  • @ Randolph Beer:
    • Nov 7 - Gin Rummy
      It doesn’t get more classic than this! Remove cards from your hand by forming sets (same number, different suit) and runs (same suit, consecutive numbers) of three or more. Go out first to screw over your opponent. You know how this goes! (Learn more)
    • Nov 14 - ST&S Episode 4
      Title: Monk-y Business / Core Mechanic: Go Cuckoo
    • Nov 28 - Complicated Board Game the Card Game
      I’m So Meta, Even This Acronym! CBGtCG is a card game about learning the rules to complicated board games, and figuring it out as you go along. If it doesn’t make sense, that’s probably by (over)design. (Learn more)
    • Dec 5 - ST&S Finale
      Title: Monk-y Business / Core Mechanic: Funemployed
    • Dec 12 - Best Of
      Bringing back our favorite games from this season!
